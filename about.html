ABOUT ME

01 / Mindset

Why I Love Soccer

Soccer pushes me to be my best every day. I love competing, working with my teammates, and constantly improving my game. Every match is an opportunity to grow, make an impact, and help my team succeed.

02 / Approach

My Playing Style

I stay calm under pressure, communicate with my teammates, and take pride in shutting down attackers. I read the game well, win tackles, regain possession, and look to start the attack with smart, composed distribution.

03 / Tools

My Strengths

My biggest strengths are my work ethic, speed, one-on-one defending, and positioning. I stay disciplined, compete with intensity, and give 100% for my team every time I step on the field.

04 / Growth

Areas I’m Developing

I’m always working to improve my passing consistency, distribution under pressure, and overall soccer IQ so I can continue to grow as a player.

05 / Direction

Career Goals

My goal is to compete at the highest level possible, earn my degree, and continue pursuing opportunities to play professionally after college.
